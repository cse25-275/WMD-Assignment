Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the safari packages?

All packages include accommodation, meals, game drives, professional guides, park fees, and airport transfers. Specific inclusions vary by package.

Do I need a visa to visit Botswana?

Many nationalities receive visa-free entry for up to 90 days. Check with the Botswana embassy for your specific requirements.

What should I pack for a safari?

Light, neutral-colored clothing, sun protection, binoculars, camera equipment, and any personal medications. We'll send a detailed packing list upon booking.

Is malaria prophylaxis necessary?

Botswana is a low-risk malaria area, with risk varying by season and location. Consult your doctor about prophylaxis before travel.
