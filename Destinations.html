Okavango Delta

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the world's largest inland deltas. Experience pristine waterways, abundant wildlife, and unique ecosystems.

Wildlife Highlights
  • Elephants
  • Lions
  • Leopards
  • Wild Dogs
  • Hippos
  • Crocodiles
Best Time to Visit
May to September


Chobe National Park

Home to one of Africa's largest elephant populations with over 50,000 individuals. The Chobe River creates spectacular wildlife viewing popportunities.

Wildlife Highlights
  • Elephants
  • Buffalo
  • Lions
  • Giraffes
  • Zebras
  • Waterbuck
Best Time to Visit
April to October


Kalahari Transfonteir Park

One of the largest game reserves in the world, featuring vast grasslands and seasonal wildlife migrations in this semi-arid landscape.

Wildlife Highlights
  • Black-maned Lions
  • Cheetahs
  • Brown Hyenas
  • Gemsbok
  • Springbok
  • Meerkats
Best Time to Visit
December to April
